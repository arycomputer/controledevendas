# **App Name**: VendasControl

## Core Features:

- Customer Registration: Allow users to register customer details, including name, contact information, and address.
- Parts Inventory: Enable the creation and management of a parts inventory with details like name, description, price, and quantity.
- Sales Tracking: Track sales transactions by associating customers with purchased parts and recording the date, quantity, and total amount.
- Reporting and Analytics: Generate sales reports and analytics based on date ranges, customers, or parts to gain insights into sales performance.
- Customer Search: Search for existing customers by name, phone number or document.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and professionalism in sales management.
- Background color: Light Gray (#EEEEEE) to ensure a clean, uncluttered interface, promoting readability.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF9800) for highlighting key actions and interactive elements, ensuring calls to action are clearly visible.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif for a balance of modern readability and approachability in all text elements.
- Use flat, minimalistic icons to represent different functionalities, ensuring visual consistency and easy recognition.
- Implement a responsive design with a clear grid structure to accommodate different screen sizes, optimizing usability across devices.
